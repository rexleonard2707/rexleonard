HI!!

Welcome to Streetwear Brand! We are a small but growing business that specializes in providing

our customers with the highest quality streetwear. Our goal is to provide our customers with the latest

trends and styles in order to keep them looking their best. We strive to create a unique look for

each individual that reflects their personality and style. At Streetwear Brand,

we’re passionate about creating and selling quality streetwear that offers a modern,

stylish and comfortable fit. We believe in offering our customers

a wide range of options and designs to choose from. From our lightweight hoodies and joggers,

to our graphic tees and denim, our collections are

designed to keep you looking and feeling your best.

Shop

Located in the centre of Munich, the store opened its doors in March 2013.

Since then it serves as headquarter for all PP related activities, offering in

house products next to selected items of other labels (music / garment / print).

In regular intervals it gives space to instore sessions and lectures by

local and international guests.
